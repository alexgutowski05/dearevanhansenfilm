What is Dear Evan Hansen?

July 9th, 2019 marks a giant milestone in musical theater history. Dear Evan Hansen, the Tony Award-winning musical 3 years in a row, was first performed on this day. With a hit soundtrack by the composing legends, Benj Pasek and Justin Paul, the musical gets sold out moments after the tickets go on sale, and the soundtrack has 1.2 million monthly listeners on Spotify. This original musical has a powerful story accompanied by a novel relased by the creaters of the musical.
